SPARX MCA 2022

Master of Computer Application (MCA) under faculty of Engineering course is affiliated to University of Savitribai Phule Pune and approved by AICTE, is established in the year 2007. MCA is a PG Program under engineering faculty with intake capacity of 60. The MCA Course having 4 semesters (2 Year) out of that last semester is for internship. In 4th semester students have to do the project in industry. Our aim is to provide our students the all-time learning and management skills that make easy them to nurture in their professions and move ahead to positions of responsibility by effective Industry-Institute Interaction. The Department of MCA Engineering has fully equipped with class rooms having LCD Projectors, high speed internet, well equipped labs. All labs have latest powerful Servers, Workstations, Printers, software’s, etc. connected in Wi-Fi. The department has established a good reputation and competency over the years.
